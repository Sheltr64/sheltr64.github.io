Recieving mail has been a nightmare

At the start of the school year, one thing that I've been frothing at the mouth at is the copious discounts that being a college student provides (Amazon Prime, YouTube Premium, FREE NYT and WSJ, and more) so I was quick to snatch up any deal I could get my hands on!

But, all things come at a cost; that being the post office here. Since all college students are spoiled brats (me included), they are used to ordering things through Amazon. While their infestructure might have been good for high volume a decade ago, it's nothing in comparason to today's consumer habits. Many of the people I've met have this exact train of thought.

  • I don't have thing
    • guess I'll take the bus to the store
    • guess I'll ask around for what I need
    • guess I'll order it off of Amazon

While convienient, it doesn't bode well for the local post. I've tried to get a Sunday paper from the NYT, and I have yet to recieve a single paper. While annoying, I can understand why the current system is so bad. The quality of living has skyrocketed in the past couple of years. While tough on my patience, I'm pretty content with how things are going now.

(wait until I have to order a perishable item, that'll be an adventure)
