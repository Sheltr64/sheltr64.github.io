I tried (and failed) to get into Pierce the Veil

My friend is a huge emo-pop fan. He's been looking for someone to talk music with for quite some time

I decided to pick up Pierce the Veils "Selfish Machines" (left) and "Collide With The Sky" (right)

I won't deny that the quality of music was there, but nothing felt really that "special" I know this is a gross misrepresentation, but I swear I was listening to My Chemical Romance, a band I found to be a lot more interesting as a whole. I digress, the whole focus on the entry should be on the music. Which is great! I loved the over top preformances of Vic Fuentes and his extremely high vocal registers. Although after my 2 hour long marithon, it got to the point where the insrumentals started to all blend together.

for me, the best songs are:

  • Caraphernelia
  • King For A Day
  • Hell Above
  • Besitos

I got diagnosed with a huge case of the "Cool but its not for me's" which is a shame, because the same hyperdramatic lyrics that make my eyes roll now, would have been happily welcomed by 14 year old me. It definitely is a solid outfit that I do respect.

overall. 7/10
